Senior Data Scientist

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our growing machine learning team.
You will work on building recommendation systems, forecasting models, and NLP pipelines
that power our core product experience.

Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field
- 4+ years of experience in data science or applied machine learning
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or PyTorch
- Experience with NLP techniques including TF-IDF, word embeddings, and transformer models
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Solid understanding of statistics, A/B testing, and experimental design

Preferred Qualifications
- PhD in a quantitative field
- Experience with Apache Spark, Airflow, or dbt
- Experience deploying ML models to production (MLflow, SageMaker, Vertex AI)
- Contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience with recommender systems at scale

Responsibilities
- Design and train machine learning models for recommendations and forecasting
- Collaborate with engineering to productionize models
- Run experiments and analyze results
- Mentor junior data scientists
- Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders
